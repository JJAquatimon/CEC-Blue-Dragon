Choose Your Game Mode

Select how you want to play the Blue Dragon Snake game

Single Player Mode

  • Control the blue dragon with arrow keys
  • Dragon can pass through walls and appear on the opposite side
  • Game speeds up over time
  • One life - game ends if you bite yourself
  • Score increases with each food eaten

Two Player Mode

  • Player 1: Arrow keys | Player 2: WASD keys
  • Dragons can collide but won't die from hitting each other
  • Each player has 2 lives
  • Losing a life reduces dragon size by half
  • Highest score wins at the end
